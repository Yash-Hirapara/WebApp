About Us

The way to the soul is through a cup of tea!

Tea is not just a beverage in India-it is an emotion. It is the ultimate remedy that not only relieves stress but also soothes the soul. From chai stalls in the smallest street corners to high-end malls, Indians are seen sipping on a hot cup of tea everywhere.

We understand the importance of a good cup of tea- we really do! At Refreshing Hub Tea, we ensure that every cup served is freshly brewed and meets the hallmarks of taste and quality every single time. The secret to a good cup of tea is the love of the person who makes it. Therefore, we have a team of experienced chai makers who ensure that each cup is filled with love!
